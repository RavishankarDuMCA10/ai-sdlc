# Interview Questions for Accounting (Car Rental System)

1. What are the key financial records you need the system to maintain for car rentals (e.g. rental income, expenses, deposits)?
2. Are there specific accounting standards or regulations that the car rental system must adhere to for financial transactions?
3. How should the system handle invoicing for rentals (individual, corporate, recurring)?
4. What are the requirements for recording and managing customer deposits (refundable, non-refundable)?
5. Should the system integrate with existing accounting software? If so, which ones?
6. What financial reports would you need from the system (profit/loss, outstanding payments, tax calculations)?
7. Should the system support multiple currencies or tax regimes?
8. How should the system manage refunds, adjustments, and chargebacks?
9. Are there specific internal controls or approval workflows required for financial transactions?
10. What data retention policies do you require for accounting records in the car rental line of business?
