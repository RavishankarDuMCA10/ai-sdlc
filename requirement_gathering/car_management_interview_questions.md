# Interview Questions for Car Management (Service, Delivery, Pickup) (Car Rental System)

1. How should the car inventory for rentals be managed in the system?
2. What information about each car should be tracked (location, condition, service schedule, availability)?
3. What is the process for assigning cars to rental bookings?
4. How should car service and maintenance schedules be handled? Should automatic reminders be sent?
5. How should the system manage car pickup and return logistics (locations, times, damage checks)?
6. What documentation is required at pickup and return (photos, damage reports, signatures)?
7. How should the system record incidents (accidents, breakdowns) during a rental period?
8. Should the system support delivery or pickup at customer-chosen locations? How is this managed?
9. Are there any integration needs with existing fleet management or GPS systems?
10. What reports are needed on vehicle utilization, downtime, and maintenance?
