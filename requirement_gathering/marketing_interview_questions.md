# Interview Questions for Marketing (Car Rental System)

1. What marketing channels does the company currently use for promoting car sales? Which of these should be leveraged for car rentals?
2. What kind of promotional campaigns do you envision for car rentals (e.g. seasonal offers, loyalty programs, partnerships)?
3. Do you need to segment customers for targeted car rental marketing? Which segmentation attributes are important?
4. What data or metrics do you need from the car rental system to evaluate campaign effectiveness?
5. Are there any upsell or cross-sell opportunities you foresee between car sales and rentals?
6. Do you require integration with external marketing tools or platforms (email, social media, ad platforms)?
7. How would you like to manage car rental pricing and promotions in the system? Should dynamic pricing be supported?
8. Should the system support referral or affiliate programs for car rentals?
9. What kind of reporting or dashboard features do you need from the system for marketing purposes?
10. How should customer feedback and reviews for car rentals be collected and utilized in marketing?
