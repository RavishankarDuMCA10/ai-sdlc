# Interview Questions for Payment (Car Rental System)

1. Which payment methods should be supported (credit/debit cards, wallets, cash, bank transfer, etc.)?
2. Are there preferred payment gateways or providers the company works with?
3. How should the system handle partial payments or installments for rentals?
4. Is pre-authorization required for card payments at the time of booking? How should this be managed?
5. What are the criteria for processing refunds or cancellations?
6. What level of security and compliance is required for payment data (PCI DSS, GDPR, etc.)?
7. Are there special payment flows for corporate or repeat customers?
8. Should the system automatically reconcile payments with bookings and invoices?
9. How should the system handle payment failures or disputes?
10. What notifications or alerts are needed for payment processing (success, failure, chargebacks)?
