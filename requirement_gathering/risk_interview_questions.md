# Interview Questions for Risk (Car Rental System)

1. What types of risks are associated with car rentals that the system should help manage (e.g. fraud, theft, non-payment, damage)?
2. Are there specific customer verification and KYC requirements?
3. How should the system evaluate and record customer eligibility for renting (age, license validity, blacklist, credit checks)?
4. What is the process for handling damages or traffic violations during a rental period?
5. How should insurance policies be managed and integrated into the system? Are there different levels of coverage?
6. What are the requirements for recording and resolving disputes related to rentals?
7. Should the system track and report on high-risk customers or transactions?
8. Are there any external risk management services or APIs to be integrated?
9. What logging or audit trail capabilities are needed to support risk investigations?
10. What notifications or escalations are required for risk-related events?
